This memoir is by no means an attempt to overshadow your work, but to highlight it. And although I didn’t solely rely on The Game and Rules of the Game, they were my main reference, and an eye-opening experience to a world of opportunity.

So here I am, 23 years old, sitting at my place wondering why I still suck ass with women after reading The Art of Seduction and becoming the ideal lover archetype (it did get me places… just not where I wanted to go). Reading The Game was actually ChatGPT’s suggestion.

Your book, your manual, the leather cover, and the golden lines - gave me an equal fighting chance — a way to become visible. I may not have your literary edge in these field reports, but I did document the messy, nonlinear, ugly reality of progress.
