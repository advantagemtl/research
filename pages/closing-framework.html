The Three Step Prospecting Turnaround Framework

MUST-DOs: Handle reflex responses • Brush offs • Objections

For reflex responses - brush offs - and objections during prospecting, you'll deploy a simple but powerful three-step framework (Figure 10.2):

  • 1 - Ledge

  • 2 - Distrupt

  • 3 - Ask

Ledge

A Ledge : Memorized , automatic response to perceived or real rejection that does not require you to think. It gives your logical brain the moment it needs to catch up, rise above distruptive emotions, and gain control.

Distrupt

Your prospect's brain (specifically amygdala) ignores patterns and is pulled to abnormalities - different unexpected - bright, shiny things. Pattern painting - doing the unexpected - is how you flip the prospect's buyer's script, turn them around and pull them towards you here are some examples :

To Open the Call

Hi , Do you have a minute to talk to me this is a sales call ?

Hi , Would you throw your phone off the window if I told you this is a sales call ?

BO : What is this regarding
You : It's your favourtie subject [Pos Machines - Marketing] ETC....

Examples of objection handling using Ledge distrupt

Ex 1 [POS Sales Related]

BO : We're happy with our current vendor
You: Awesome. If you already have great pricing and service, it wouldn’t make sense to change. All I want to do today is share some info — you’ll see exactly how much you’re paying and what it could be. If we’re not a good fit, no worries — at least it gives you a competitive quote to keep your current provider honest.

Ex 2 [General Example]

BO : Im super busy at the moment
You : That's exactly why I called, I figured you'd be, and I wanna find a time that's more convenient to you.

Ex 3 [POS Sales System]

BO : Just send me some information
You : I can definetly send you an email but can I ask you a question? If I was able to [Give you a company with a solid reputation - Great service - and the best Pricing in the market] Would you be willing to consider? [Yes] -> Excellent I just need to know if your eligible Who are you processing with (Moneris FirstData Global TD) and how many terminals you have?

Ex 4 - [Not interested - General]

BO : We're not interested - I'm not interested
You : Sir our best customers say the same thing as you did, and then end up thanking me for calling them later on

Ex 5 - [Not interested] POS Specefic

BO : We're not interested - I'm not interested
You : You know, that's what a lot of my clients said, until they learned how much I could save them, look I don't even know if youre eligible to benefit from our program, but hypothetically speaking, If I could able to give you [A company with a solid reputation - the best service ever - and an unbeatable offer] would you keep an open mind and take a look at it?

Ex 6 - We do this in house

BO: We do this in house
You: That's exactly why I called. Most of my clients have in-house programs, and they choose to work with me to complement what they already have. I don't know if we're a good fit — and if you decide we're not, that's completely okay. Would it make sense to do a quick discovery call so I can understand your current situation, and then come back to you with a presentation of how I can — and will — help? If this isn’t a good time, you can just tell me when to call you back.



Ask

Back to lesson ONE, to get what you WANT, you must ASK for what you want, you may deliver the perfect distruptive turnaround, but if you don't ask ,you wont get the outcome you desire.
The ask step is where most prospecting RBO turnarounds fall apart, the salesperson hesitates and waits for the prospects do the work, they don't and they won't.
you must Control your EMOTIONS and ASK AGAIN, assumptively and assertively, for what you want. without hesitation, directly following your turnaround script.
