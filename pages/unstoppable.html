Is there a better way? Could there be a better way that the one we’re using today? (There always is) knowing what we now know, would we know , would we start to do it this way again? What would we do differently? That keeps your mind

These questions are slapping people in the face. What are we trying to do? How are we going to do it? Is it working? If not, why not? What are our assumptions? What would we do if our assumptions ? What would we do if our assumptions were wrong? What if we had no limitations? What if we had an unlimited budget and we could do anything we wanted? What would we do differently if they were to come in and shoot us in the head if we hadn’t accomplished the goal in six months? What changes would we make immediately? Put the pressure on, and people come up with incredible ideas?
